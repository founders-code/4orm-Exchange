Project Samara reference

The Bank of Canada's Project Samara pilot concluded March 2025: a C$100M tokenized bond issued by Export Development Canada, settled token-for-token against wholesale CBDC (W-CAD) on a permissioned distributed ledger. First production proof of atomic settlement against central bank money in Canada.
