What 4ormEx is, and is not

4ormEx is a design preview of a planned permissioned trading venue (Pillar 02) inside the future 4orm Finance regulated control plane. Today, 4ormEx is software. It is not a registered marketplace, dealer, exchange, money services business, or bank in any Canadian jurisdiction. No applications have been filed.

Operating a marketplace for securities is a registrable activity in Canada under National Instruments 21-101, 23-101, 24-102 and 31-103. 4ormEx intends to seek marketplace recognition and dealer registration before operating any live trading. Until that registration exists, no order, custody, deposit, or payment is being offered, accepted, or solicited through this site.

Every figure, identifier, price, balance, hash, and counterparty shown here is simulated, fictional, or illustrative. Where real institutions are named, they are external precedents only and are not partners, counterparties, or integrations of 4ormEx.
