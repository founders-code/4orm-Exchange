Asset Class · Real Estate

Tokenized real estate, three sub-classes.

Illustrative real-estate category. The venue is designed to support three sub-classes inside one umbrella: tokenized REITs (publicly comparable, transparency-tiered), direct commercial real estate (single-asset, sponsor-led), and residential mortgage pools (diversified, distribution-rule-embedded). Issuers and sponsors are described by category only. No real assets are listed and no offering is being made.

Sub-class 01

Tokenized REITs

REIT-structured tokens with publicly comparable disclosure. Buyers treat these closer to equities: NAV-anchored pricing, quarterly distributions, transparency-tiered reporting. Designed for institutional buyers who want diversified real-estate exposure without single-asset concentration.

Direct CRE

Single-asset commercial real estate offerings led by a private sponsor. Trades closer to private credit: sponsor-led structure, asset-specific risk, accredited-only distribution. Designed for buyers who want concentrated, asset-specific exposure with sponsor alignment.

Sub-class 03

Residential pools

Pooled residential exposure: tokenized mortgage pools, build-to-rent funds, and multi-family residential. Trades like private credit with embedded distribution rules. Designed for buyers who want diversified residential exposure without direct property ownership.
