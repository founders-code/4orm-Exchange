Asset Class · Carbon

Tokenized carbon credits, two tiers.

Illustrative carbon-credit category. The venue is designed to support two tiers in parallel: compliance carbon (regulator-issued allowances under capped systems) and voluntary carbon (project-based credits from verified offset and removal programs). Each tier has different buyers, different price discovery, and different on-chain attestation needs. Issuers and registries are described by category only. No real markets are integrated and no credits are being offered.

Tier 1 · Compliance carbon

Regulator-issued allowances

Allowances issued under federally or provincially capped emissions systems. Buyers are emitters with statutory compliance obligations. Trading is between regulated participants in the cap-and-trade system. Tokenization is a representation layer; underlying allowances remain in the regulator's registry.

Tier 2 · Voluntary carbon

Project-based removals & reductions

Project-based credits from verified offset and removal programs purchased by corporates and asset managers with voluntary net-zero mandates. Tokenization represents a retired or retirable credit from a recognized registry. Higher diligence burden than compliance; the venue is designed to surface methodology, vintage, registry, and additionality on every listing.

What the venue is designed to surface per listing

  • Issuer category, never a name. Compliance carbon: regulator and cap participant role. Voluntary: project developer category and region.
  • Methodology and registry. Public methodology code (Puro, Verra VCS, Gold Standard, ICR, CSA-derived). No proprietary methodology is on-chain only.
  • Vintage and project lifecycle stage. Pre-issuance, issued, retired, or retirable.
  • Independent verifier category. Type of third-party verifier without naming a specific firm.
  • Co-benefits (voluntary only). Biodiversity, community, water (for buyers with SDG-aligned mandates).
  • Retirement record. On-chain proof of retirement keyed to the underlying registry serial range.

Buyer profiles the category is designed for

  • Compliance carbon: Canadian large emitters with OBPS, Quebec or Alberta TIER obligations; aviation operators under CORSIA; cross-border emitters with EU ETS exposure.
  • Voluntary carbon (institutional): Asset managers with SBTi / net-zero mandates, pensions with PRI commitments, corporate treasury teams retiring credits against scope-1 / scope-3 emissions.
  • Voluntary carbon (sovereign / Crown): Crown corporations and federal agencies with internal carbon pricing or net-zero operations targets.
  • All buyers gated: KYC, NI 31-103 dealer status (where applicable), permissioned wallet, sanctions screen. No retail access on the venue.
